Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will be often seen in flocks of 20-30 birds
  2. Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet at an average speed of 77.6 mph.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Cicada

  • Cicadas spend most of their life under the ground.
  • Cicadas can reach 0.79 to 3 inches in length.
  • Cicadas are one of the loudest insects on the planet.

Panda

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca.
  3. Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm.

Chameleon

  • Chameleons can look at two different things at once.
  • When a chameleon is cold, it will become darker in color.
  • The panther chameleon changes its color to yellow or red when it’s angry.

Camel

  1. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  2. Thick pads of skin on their chest and knees make them comfortably sit in very hot sand.
  3. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse.

Teddy

  • A teddy bear is a stuffed toy in the form of a bear.
  • Teddy bears are among the most popular gifts for children.
  • Teddy Bears are usually small and soft and are found all over the world.
